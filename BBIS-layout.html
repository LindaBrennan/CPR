RBC is possibility
2016 Corporate Partnership Report

Welcome

Welcome message set in Avenir, 20/28, grey (77%black). Approximately 112 words (half of what in last year’s print iteration). Generosity of RBC and its 23,360 employees and retirees across Canada raised more than $19.4 million for United Way Centraide and other registered charities. Thank you for this tremendous support.

As Canada’s largest financial institution, RBC understands that investments in strong, prosperous communities lead to healthy outcomes for people and business. This philosophy guides RBC’s success as a leading corporate citizen and United Way Centraide’s largest private-sector supporter.

United Way Centraide shares RBC’s deep commitment to improving lives locally and creating possibility for individuals and families in the same communities where RBC employees live, work and raise their families. We understand the issues that matter most to RBC—including helping.

Recognition

RBC is a leading corporate citizen that makes a meaningful and impactful difference in communities where RBC’s clients, employees and retirees live, work and raise their families. United Way Centraide is proud to recognize the RBC brand through a variety of high-reach media channels.

Here are a few examples of how United Way Centraide celebrated its partnership with RBC in 2014:

Awards


This is a caption about the photo

In 2014, United Way was proud to recognize RBC in Calgary, Oakville and Toronto with awards celebrating their Campaign achievements. Examples include:

  • Calgary: Division Recognition Award – Finance Division
  • Oakville: Spirit Award – Finance Division
  • Toronto: Spirit Award – Major Banks – Campaign Team
  • And a national “Thanks a Million” award, which recognizes and celebrates leading Canadian corporations, employers and labour organizations that—together with their employees and members—raised $1 million or more in support of United Way.

Thank You

United Way Centraide extends its sincere thanks and appreciation to RBC and its remarkably dedicated employees across the country.

Our philanthropic partnership is a perfect fit for two organizations that share a national footprint and a deep commitment to improving lives locally.

RBC distinguishes itself as an exemplary corporate citizen that invests in the future of our communities—and our young people. United Way Centraide is proud to share this vision and support programs and services across the country that strengthen individuals, families and entire neighbourhoods both today—and in the future.

United Way Centraide looks forward to continuing our valued partnership in the years to come.

Thank you.
